Unclouded: Political Literacy For Kids

Many individuals between the ages of 12 and 19 perceive politics as inaccessible because information is seldom presented in an approachable and engaging way for youth. The negative perception leads to alienation, creates lower civic engagement, and often an avoidance of political discussions altogether. Our goal is to address the insufficient political education among youth and increase the incentive to engage in political spaces and fulfill civic responsibilities.
