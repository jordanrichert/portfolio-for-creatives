  • About Me

    A highly customizable portfolio starter template with a big hero for your branding and intro. The work page is based on CSS Grid. To start, choose a version of the work page. Customize the header and footer and copy it to each page.

    Beginners can add their own logo, custom typography, custom colours, project images, custom hover states, custom cursor, and more. Experiment with animated GIFs to showcase your work, animated SVGs, and animated gradients!

    Advanced coders can add their own masonry, responsive nav, Lightbox, typewriter effect, parallax effect, custom JS effects, and easter eggs!

    Consider adding a play page, sketchbook page, and resume. Don't forget to customize your 404 Error Page and optimize your page titles and descriptions for SEO!
