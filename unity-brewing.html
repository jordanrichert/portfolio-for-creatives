Unity Brewing: Sustainable Non-Alcoholic Beer

Packaging Design, Branding and Positioning, Illustration, Merchandise and Collateral Design

The non-alcoholic aisle is a sea of greenwashed branding and corporate fizz—vague wellness buzzwords up front, damning sustainability practices just out of view. More than forty of the biggest non-alcoholic options can be traced back to the same multinational conglomerate.

People are seeking out mindful consumption and sober-friendly options more than ever, so why does everything still taste like compromise? If folks are out there looking for something better, why not actually give it to them?

Unity Brewing answers that call. Crafted with 100% sustainably sourced ingredients from British Columbia, Unity brews good beer for the sober, the sober-curious seeker-outers, and everyone in between—no cutting corners. Keeping it real from production to distribution means responsible labour, local suppliers, and an unwavering respect for the unceded Indigenous land Unity calls home. Doing better isn’t a bonus—it’s the baseline.

Unity Brewing embraces a flexible illustration system that celebrates connection, community, and craft through playful, hand-drawn details. Each flavour pairs botanicals native to British Columbia with simple moments of human connection, weaving together people and place to reflect our shared relationship with the land. It visually reinforces the brand’s core promise: 100% sustainable, locally made, non-alcoholic beer, crafted by good people for good people. The visual language pushes back against the sterile, mass-produced feel of mainstream non-alc brands. It’s intentional, grounded, and unmistakably human.
